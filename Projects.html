My Projects:

Assistance League: Operation School Bell

For our final project in my Databases class, we were instructed to construct a functioning mock database for the Assistance League, a non-profit organization that is dedicated to serving the community. Their program, Operation School Bell, assists children and provides them with clothing for the school year. Our team utilized flowcharts, SQL, and ERDs to construct our database that the Assistance League could use to keep track of inventory.
